Features

Built for the story of a life,
not a feed of it.

Organized by age

Every photo, video, and note falls into place along one continuous rail — by year and by age, from day one onward.

Photos & video, together

Capture moments exactly as they happened. Snap a photo or take a video — both live side by side in the same entry.

Import from Instagram

Already have years of memories on Instagram? Bring your posts straight in — no re-uploading, no starting from zero.

Comments, not likes

Family can leave a note on any memory. No public likes, no algorithm — just the people who matter, replying back.

Invite the whole family

Bring in parents, grandparents, or close friends as editors who can add moments, or viewers who just get to watch it grow.

Pick a look that fits

Choose a theme for each timeline — from clean and minimal to warm and classic — and make it feel like yours.

Turn it into a keepsake book

Pick any stretch of the timeline and lay it out as a printable photo book — a memory you can actually hold.

Works offline

Your timeline keeps working on a plane, in the car, wherever you are — everything syncs the moment you're back online.

Private by default

There's no public feed and no discovery page. A timeline is only ever visible to the people you personally invite.

How it works

From first entry to
a life story, in four steps.

  1. 01

    Start a timeline

    Give it a name and a starting date — a birthday, or just today.

  2. 02

    Add a moment

    Drop in a photo, a video, and a few words whenever something's worth keeping.

  3. 03

    Invite your people

    Bring in family as editors, or share view-only access with anyone else.

  4. 04

    Watch it build itself

    Every entry files itself under the right year and age — no scrolling to sort things out.

Privacy first

Where memories matter more than likes.

Not everything needs to be public to be worth keeping.

My Timeline was built as the opposite of a social feed. There's no algorithm, no likes, and no way for anyone to find a timeline unless you send them the invite yourself.

  • ✓ No public profiles or discovery
  • ✓ You choose viewer or editor access, person by person
  • ✓ Remove anyone's access at any time
  • ✓ Your media stays tied to your account, not an ad platform
