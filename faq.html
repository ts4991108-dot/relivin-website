FAQ — Common Questions

Answers to the things people ask us most.

What is Relivin?

Relivin is a private, invite-only app for keeping the story of a life as it happens — photos, videos, and notes, automatically organized by year and age rather than dumped into one long camera roll.

Who can see my timeline?

Only the people you personally invite. There's no public profile, no discovery page, and no way for anyone to stumble onto a timeline — it's shared strictly by invitation.

What's the difference between a viewer and an editor?

Viewers can open a timeline and see everything on it. Editors can also add new entries and comments. You choose the level for each person you invite, and can change or remove their access at any time.

Can I keep more than one timeline?

Yes — keep a separate timeline for each child, or one for yourself, and switch between them from your home screen.

What happens if I remove someone's access?

They immediately lose the ability to view or edit that timeline. Anything they already added stays on the timeline unless you remove it separately.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, at any time, from My Account → Delete Account in the app. This permanently removes your personal information. Any timeline only you belong to is deleted along with its photos and videos; a timeline you share with others is handed off to another member instead, so shared family history isn't lost. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Relivin free?

Relivin is currently in early access as we build with a small group of families. Pricing hasn't been finalized — join the waitlist and we'll let you know as soon as that's decided and access opens up.

What devices does it work on?

Relivin is a mobile app for iPhone and Android.
